## Key Features

### Core Health Features
- **Real-time Health Analysis**: HealthKit integration with background monitoring and updates
- **Personalized Questionnaire**: Comprehensive health assessment with validation and state management
- **Advanced Life Impact Calculations**: Scientific research-based algorithms with interaction effects
- **Life Expectancy Projection**: Dynamic total life projection with confidence intervals
- **Interaction Effect Processing**: Complex multi-metric health interaction calculations
- **Offline Operation**: Comprehensive caching strategy for uninterrupted use

### Visualization & UI
- **Dual Battery System**: 
  - Impact Battery: Short-term health habit impacts
  - Projection Battery: Total projected lifespan visualization
- **Glass Theme Interface**: Apple Liquid Glass effects with multiple material types
- **3D Visual Effects**: Enhanced depth and transformation animations
- **Accessibility Support**: Full VoiceOver and accessibility feature compliance
- **Battery-Themed Visualization**: Familiar energy level indicators following Apple design patterns

### User Experience
- **Engagement System**: Streak tracking with milestone rewards and notifications
- **Personalized Recommendations**: AI-driven health insights and actionable tips
- **Feature Flag System**: Gradual rollout and A/B testing capabilities
- **Frictionless UX**: "Little yesses" conversion principle throughout the app
- **Performance Optimization**: Launch optimization and efficient background processing
- **Private Processing**: All health calculations on-device with no external data sharing
